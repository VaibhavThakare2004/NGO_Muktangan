About Muktangan

Our Story of Compassion

Founded with a vision to create a world where every mother and child receives the care they deserve, Muktangan has been a beacon of hope for underprivileged communities.

"Healthy families build healthy nations."

Muktangan

"A touch of care can change a life."

Our Vision

To envision a future where every mother and child, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality healthcare, education, and a nurturing environment, ensuring a healthy and prosperous life.

Our Mission

To ensure safe childbirth, promote maternal health, and provide continuous support for both mother and child from birth through early childhood, especially in underserved communities.

Comprehensive Care

From prenatal to postnatal support, we're with you every step.

Community Focus

Reaching the most vulnerable communities with essential services.

Holistic Approach

Addressing physical, emotional, and educational needs.
