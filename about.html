About Muktangan

Our Story of Compassion

Founded with a vision to create a world where every mother and child receives the care they deserve, Muktangan has been a beacon of hope for underprivileged communities.

Our Vision

To build a healthier and empowered India, where every mother and child thrives â€” free from preventable illness, enriched by awareness, and supported by compassionate, holistic care.

Our Mission

At Muktangan, we are committed to:

Health Care

Safeguarding maternal and child health through accessible healthcare, education, and nutritional support.

Disease

Eliminating thalassemia via community outreach, screening programs, and genetic counseling.

Awareness

Raising cancer awareness for early detection and timely intervention, especially in underserved communities.

Our Impact

Making a Tangible Difference

At Muktangan, our commitment translates into real-world impact. Here's how we've been making a difference in the lives of individuals and communities.

100+

Lives Touched

Screenings Conducted
